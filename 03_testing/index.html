## Week 3: ### Monday: We continued to test the three solar cookers (the GoSun/small solar cooker, the All Season Solar Cooker(yellow solar cooker, and the LiFuJunDong 1800W Solar Cooker/the large solar cooker). It was sunny outside, so the large solar cooker was successfully able to boil water after a reasonable amount of time. The other two were much slower, with the small solar cooker doing better than the yellow solar cooker. I have included pictures from using the FLIR camera: ![The large solar cooker](./Large_testing_3.JPG) ![The small solar cooker](./Small_testing_3.JPG) ![The yellow solar cooker](./Yellow_testing_3.JPG) All three pictures were taken at 4:04pm. In each, I used FLIR to measure the warmest, coldest, and a middling temperature on the surface of the solar cookers. We once again noticed that the smallest solar cooker is the only one with the warmest spot on the mirror.
