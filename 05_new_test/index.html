## Week 5: ### Monday: No class: Indiginous People's Day ### Wednesday: We tested our new prototype reflector, while I was able to take pictures with our FLIR camera. It was not able to boil water, unfortunately. We hypothesized that this was due to the smaller surface area of the mirror. I also noticed that the acrylic mirror was actually warmer than the large reflector we tested on Week 3. The warmest spot on our prototype reflector was 30.6 degrees Celsius, while the large reflector from week 3 had a warmest spot of 26.1 degrees Celsius. This might also be a contributing factor for the water not boiling. The surface temperature of the pot was also very different (57.2 degrees Celsius today vs 71.3 degrees Celsius with the large reflector). We therefore must raise the surface temperature of the pot by around 15 degrees Celsius in order to boil water.
